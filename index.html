§ 01

Research Interests

🚗

Shared Mobility Systems

Analyzing car-sharing, bike-sharing, and e-scooter systems through high-resolution vehicle-level data. Understanding usage patterns, spatial distribution, and integration with public transport.

🗺️

Geospatial Methods

Developing feature-based approaches for spatial analysis using GPS trajectories. Monte Carlo feature selection, clustering algorithms, and visualization techniques for mobility pattern recognition.

📊

Urban Analytics

Processing large-scale mobility datasets to understand travel behavior, trip derivation, and utilization patterns in urban environments. Data-driven insights for sustainable transportation planning.

Current Project: ReFuMoLab Garching

Real-world laboratory under the MCube cluster establishing a testbed for innovative mobility solutions on the TUM Garching campus. Analyzing mobility patterns from shared vehicles and contributing to sustainable campus mobility strategies.
